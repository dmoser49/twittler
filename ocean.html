Ocean

Ordinary meaning

Dreaming of might oceans depict that the dreamer is well equipped with different deep and powerful emotions, just like the waves of the ocean. Water dreams, especially of the Ocean, are commonly observed in all the humans. This happens because we are basically made up of water and Ocean contains water the most. Nothing can be more beautiful and deep as well as scary because of being vast, than the ocean.

For the male gender: Men lack expressing their emotions comfortably. Therefore, they are observed to have more of such dreams as they do not have any other way of expressing their internal emotions. Due to this, the male gender must find ways to get rid of the problem that is in his hands so that he doesn’t drown in the Ocean which has been constructed by his own emotions. Productive expression, in such a case, helps for the male gender.

For the female gender: Women have powerful intuitions and they believe in expressing their emotions, especially when things go wrong in their lives. Generally, this happens because they do too much and hence they require taking care of their emotions in a productive manner that would help them vocalize their needs and not keep them safe and unheard, internally.

For everyone: Sea dreams or being stranded in the ocean symbolizes a feeling of being at loss in expressing the emotions. This represents that you need to be more solid in your life, at present. When such dreams occur to you, it also means that you have desires that are not possible in the real world.
